Quick Reference

Key facts at a glance
Gear Ratio
GR = Tdriven ÷ Tdriver
T = number of teeth
Mechanical Advantage
MA = Load ÷ Effort
Both in Newtons (N)
Ohm's Law
V = I × R
V=Volts · I=Amps · R=Ohms
Pressure
P = F ÷ A
P=Pa · F=N · A=m²
Velocity Ratio (Levers)
VR = Effort Arm ÷ Load Arm
Both distances in mm or m
Efficiency
η = MA ÷ VR × 100%
η = efficiency (%)
